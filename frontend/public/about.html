About Us

The Problem Solver Foundation is dedicated to addressing global challenges through innovative solutions and collaborative efforts. Our work is driven by a commitment to creating a more equitable and sustainable world for all.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Dr. Anya Sharma, the Problem Solver Foundation emerged from a deep-seated desire to tackle pressing global issues. Dr. Sharma, a renowned expert in sustainable development and social innovation, envisioned an organization that could bridge the gap between research and practical action. Inspired by her extensive fieldwork in underserved communities, she recognized the need for a collaborative platform that could bring together diverse expertise to address complex challenges effectively.

Our Mission

Our mission is to catalyze positive change by developing and implementing innovative solutions to global challenges. We focus on fostering collaboration, promoting sustainable practices, and empowering communities to create lasting impact. We believe in the power of collective action and the potential of every individual to contribute to a better future.

Areas of Expertise

Our work spans several critical areas, including sustainable development, climate action, education, and healthcare. We leverage our expertise in these domains to design and implement impactful programs that address the root causes of challenges and promote long-term solutions. Our approach is grounded in evidence-based research, community engagement, and a commitment to continuous learning and improvement.
